Privacy Policy

Test Your French is a privacy-first language assessment app. We do not collect, sell or share your personal data. Your progress and results are stored only in your browser (localStorage) and are never sent to our servers.

What we store

  • Your quiz progress and scores (in your browser only)
  • Your first name and email if you choose to save your profile (optional, local only)
  • No third-party trackers, except anonymous Google Analytics if you buy Premium (for conversion statistics only)
